I. NHẬN BIẾT (Recognition)

WRITING

Task 1: Rewrite the first sentences so that the second one means nearly the same as the first one:

1. “Why don’t you put better lock on the door, Barbara”? said John => John suggested …………………………………………………...

John suggested that Barry should put a better lock on the door.

2. Although his both legs were broken in the crash, he managed to get out of the car before it exploded. => Despite his ……………………………………………………....

Despite his both broken legs, he managed to get out of the car before it exploded.

3. I haven’t eaten this kind of food before. => This is the first …………………………………………………..

This is the time I have eaten this kind of food.

4. The architect has drawn plans for an extension to the house. => Plans …………………………………………………………….

Plans for an extension to the house have been drawn by the architect./ Plans have been drawn for an extension by the architect.

5. It isn’t necessary for you to finish by Saturday => You ………………………………………………………………

You don’t have to finish by Saturday.

6. “How many survivors are there?”, asked the journalist. => The journalist wanted to know …………………………………..

The journalist wanted to know how many survivors there were./ The number of survivors there were.

7. It was such rotten meat that it had to be thrown away. => The meat was …………………………………………………….

The meat was so rotten that it had to be thrown away.

8. It is essential that Professor Van Helsing is met at the airport. => Professor Van Helsing ……………………………………………

Professor Van Helsing must be met at the airport.

Task 2: Finish the second sentence so that it has a similar meaning to the first one, beginning with the given word(s).

1. He is late, so he cannot go with us on the ecotour to the national park today. (COULD) -> If _____________________________________________

If he weren’t late, he could go with us on the ecotour to the national park today.

2. We can see the beautiful boats when we go to the floating market. (GO) -> If _____________________________________________

If we go to the floating market, we can see the beautiful boats.

3. There are few tourists so this ecotour is cancelled. (MORE) -> If _____________________________________________

If there were more tourists, this ecotour wouldn’t be cancelled.

4. We should hurry up or we will get lost alone in the forest. (WILL) -> If _____________________________________________

If we don’t hurry up, we will get lost alone in the forest.

5. The water is rough so we are not allowed to go snorkelling. (WOULD) -> If _____________________________________________

If the water weren’t rough, we would be allowed to go snorkelling.

Task 3: Change the following sentences into active voice

1) These exercises were done well. ________________________________________.

Someone did these exercises well.

2) This dress must be washed in cold water. ________________________________________.

You must wash this dress in cold water.

3) No mistakes have been made in his composition. ________________________________________.

He has made no mistakes in his composition.

4) His painting will be exhibited for the first time by New Arts gallery. ________________________________________.

New Arts Gallery will exhibit his painting for the first time.

5) This house was built 100 years ago. ________________________________________.

They built this house 100 years ago.

6) These artificial flowers are made of silk. ________________________________________.

They make these artificial flowers of silk.

7) The lessons are being written by the students now. ________________________________________.

The students are writing the lessons now.

8) This job has to be done at once. ________________________________________.

Someone has to do this job at once.

9) These animals at the zoo are fed twice a day. ________________________________________.

The zookeepers feed these animals twice a day.

10) The operation is going to be performed by French doctors. ________________________________________.

French doctors are going to perform the operation.

Task 4: Mark the letter A, B, C or D to indicate the correct option that best fits each of the numbered blanks

1. It was the third time in six months that the bank had been held _____ . A. over B. down C. up D. out

C

2. I always run _____ of money before the end of the month. A. out B. back C. up D. down

A

3. It's taking me longer to get _____ the operation than I thought. A. through B. by C. up from D. over

D

4. I've just spent two weeks looking _____an aunt of mine who's been ill. A. at B. for C. out for D. after

D

5. I've always got _____ well with old people. A. off B. on C. in D. through

B

6. It's very cold in here. Do you mind if I turn _________ the heating? A. down B. away C. off D. on

D

7. They've _____ a new tower where that old building used to be. A. put up B. put down C. pushed up D. pushed down

A

8. Stephen always wanted to be an actor when he ________ up. A. came B. grew C. brought D. settled

B

9. The bus only stops here to ________ passengers. A. alight B. get on C. get off D. pick up

D

10. If anything urgent comes _____, you can contact me at this number. A. across B. by C. up D. round

C
